# Pinpoint Scaling — Master Dispute Template Guide

This is the primary dispute response template used by License & Scale / Pinpoint Scaling Limited. The full branded PDF is at `pinpoint-scaling-dispute-template.pdf`. Use this guide to fill in the placeholders.

## Document Structure (13 pages)

1. **Cover / Introduction** — Addresses the dispute, states the claim is unjustified
2. **Service Provider Information** — Pinpoint Scaling Ltd details (Cameron England)
3. **Overview of Purchased Service** — "Clinic Accelerator System" description:
   - Facebook campaign creation & setup
   - Monitoring & optimization
   - CRM lead nurture with patient concierge
   - Weekly check-in calls
4. **Client & Transaction Information** — Fill in ALL fields:
   - `[CLIENT NAME]` / `[CLIENT FULL PERSONAL NAME]`
   - `[CLIENT EMAIL]` / `[CLIENT PHONE]` / `[CLIENT ADDRESS]`
   - `[DISPUTE AMOUNT]` / `[REASON FOR DISPUTE]`
   - `[ID]` / `[DATE]` / `[CARD NUMBER USED]`
   - `[FINGERPRINT ID]` / `[EXPIRATION DATE]`
   - `[CARD TYPE]` / `[CARD ISSUER]` / `[POSTAL CODE]` / `[ORIGIN]`
   - CVC check / Zip check (Passed/Failed)
5. **Legally Binding Agreement** — Include:
   - `[DATE SIGNED]`
   - Screenshot of signature + date
   - Link to contract portal
   - Quote Section 2 of agreement (agent responsibilities)
6. **No Refund Policy** — Quote Section 2 of guarantee (no refunds + no chargebacks clause)
7. **Services Provided** — Must include proof for each contractual obligation:
   - **1.1.1** (Setup campaigns): Screenshot of Facebook Ads Manager showing campaigns under client's ad account, explain naming conventions
   - **1.1.2** (Optimize daily): Screenshots of ad set testing, disabled underperforming ads, explain optimization decisions
   - **1.1.3** (Follow up leads): CRM screenshots showing lead communication (SMS, email, phone)
   - **1.1.4** (Booked appointments within 30 days): Show booked appointments, lead notifications sent to client's phone
8. **Proof of Communication** — Include:
   - Check-in call recordings/screenshots
   - Fathom transcript excerpts (key quotes)
   - Slack/messaging history with client
   - Show CSM name + client on calls together
9. **Proof of Payment Authorization** — Screenshot of payment authorization
10. **Summary** — Restate all evidence, demand reversal

## Evidence Checklist (specific to this template)

- [ ] Signed contract with date (screenshot)
- [ ] Link to contract portal
- [ ] Facebook Ads Manager screenshots showing:
  - Client's ad account name visible
  - Campaign names with naming conventions
  - Results/leads generated
  - Ad sets tested + disabled (optimization proof)
- [ ] CRM screenshots showing:
  - Account created for client (with date)
  - Lead follow-up messages (SMS, email)
  - Booked appointments
  - Lead notifications sent to client phone
  - Lead source showing "facebook form lead"
- [ ] Check-in call recordings or screenshots
- [ ] Fathom meeting transcripts
- [ ] Slack/communication history
- [ ] Payment authorization screenshot
- [ ] No refund policy as shown at checkout/in contract

## Tips

- **Red box highlighting** is used throughout to draw attention to key evidence in screenshots
- Every claim from the agreement (1.1.1-1.1.4) gets its own section with dedicated proof
- The template explicitly calls out that marketing naming conventions prove professional setup
- CRM lead source attribution ("facebook form lead") ties campaigns to results
- Template emphasizes this is "fraudulent chargeback" — strong language, appropriate for friendly fraud cases
